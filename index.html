A story woven with tradition and innovation

At mmp, we don't just spin a yarn; we weave perseverance, passion, and innovation into every thread. Established in 1954 by the visionary Late Deokinandan Todi, mmp started its journey as a textile manufacturer. In 1993, we expanded our horizons with the establishment of The Technical Textile Division, marking a pivotal moment in our journey towards innovation and growth.
